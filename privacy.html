Privacy Policy: PocketCode Pro

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PocketCode Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile IDE handles your information.

2. Data We Collect & How We Use It

  • GitHub Integration (Device Flow): When you connect your GitHub account, the app receives an Access Token. This token is stored locally on your device and is used solely to authenticate your requests to clone, push, or sync repositories. We do not see or store your GitHub credentials on any external servers.
  • Local Files & Projects: All code, folders, and ZIP files you create or import are stored in your device’s internal storage or designated "Documents" folder. PocketCode Pro does not have access to your files outside of the directories you explicitly grant permission for.
  • AI Intelligence (The Codex): Our AI features use MediaPipe to run 100% locally on your device. Your code is processed in your phone’s RAM and is never sent to an external server for processing or training.
  • Google Drive/Cloud Import: If you use the "Import" feature, the app uses the system File Picker to access the specific file you select. No other cloud data is accessed.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

  • GitHub API: Used for repository synchronization.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and optional in-app purchases.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local Data: You can delete your project data at any time by deleting the files within the app or uninstalling the application.
  • GitHub Tokens: You can revoke access at any time by logging out in the app settings or via your GitHub account security settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

PocketCode Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As a coding tool, we encourage parental supervision for young learners.
